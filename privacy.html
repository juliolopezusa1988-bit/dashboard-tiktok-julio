Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Dashboard TikTok Julio application (“the App”) handles your information.

1. Information collected

The App collects only the data you explicitly authorize via TikTok’s official API, such as:

  • Video performance statistics and related metrics
  • Basic account information necessary to establish the connection

2. Use of information

Data is used solely for personal analysis, reporting, and content optimization. The App does not use the data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

3. Data sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is not disclosed except as required by law or with your explicit direction.

4. Data storage and security

Data retrieved via the API is stored locally or in your own secure environment. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

5. User control

You can revoke the App’s access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok’s account settings. If you disconnect access, the App will no longer retrieve new data.

6. Data retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for the purposes described above and may be deleted at your discretion.

7. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for use by the account owner and is not directed to children under 13. It does not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
